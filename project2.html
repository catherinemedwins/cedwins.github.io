sweetish dessert café

a brand identity and design system with website, promo package, and menu

There were several goals with this piece but I will break them down to a more concise manner; concepting and research, design, and execution.
Concepting and research was gathering relevant research to understand the problem and target the correct audience.
Designing was the use critical thinking through idea generating to make design iterations and show what the design does in context.
Execution was doing all of the above while making visual architecture aesthetically pleasing.
This project consisted of a fully flushed out design system complete with logos, applications, and an identity manual. There was also a fully branded promotional box with items like mugs, candies, hair ties, a lunch pouch sleeve, and Keurig Cups. Promotional materials like coupons, a post card, and a website were also created and implemented.
